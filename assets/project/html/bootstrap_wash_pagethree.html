Wash and Ironing

1. Sorting: We sort your clothes based on fabric type and color to prevent any damage or color bleeding.

2. Washing: We use high-quality detergents and washing techniques to ensure a thorough clean while preserving the fabric.

3. Ironing: Each garment is carefully ironed to remove wrinkles and give it a polished look.

4. Quality Check: After washing and ironing, we perform a final quality check to ensure everything is in perfect condition before returning it to you.
