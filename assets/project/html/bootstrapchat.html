Leather Care

1 .Cleaning: Use a damp cloth to wipe off any dirt or dust. For deeper cleaning, use a leather cleaner.

2 .Protection: Use a leather protector spray to guard against stains and water damage.

3 .Storage: Store leather items in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Use breathable covers to prevent moisture buildup.
